## Project Description

---

### Problem

Legal documents are often complex, difficult to understand, and expensive to create through legal professionals. Students, freelancers, and small startups frequently lack access to affordable legal assistance and struggle with both creating and interpreting legal agreements.

---

### Solution

Lawffle is an AI-powered web application that simplifies both the creation and understanding of legal documents. Users can generate complete legal documents through a guided form, while also using an AI-based simplifier to convert complex legal clauses into plain English and identify potential risks.

---

### Key Features

* AI-powered document generation (NDA, Freelance Contract, Rental Agreement, Employment Letter, Partnership Agreement)
* Legal Simplifier that explains clauses in simple language
* Risk Analyzer that flags potential issues (High / Medium / Low)
* Professional PDF document download
* Clean and user-friendly interface

---

### Tech Stack

* Frontend: Next.js (TypeScript), Tailwind CSS
* Backend: Node.js, Express.js
* AI Integration: Claude Sonnet API (Anthropic)
* Deployment: Vercel (Frontend), Render (Backend)

---

### Impact

Lawffle makes legal tools more accessible to everyday users by removing complexity and cost barriers. It empowers individuals to create and understand legal agreements quickly, helping freelancers secure fair contracts, startups protect ideas, and users make informed legal decisions without requiring expert knowledge.
